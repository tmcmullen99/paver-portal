Per-color swatch uploads

Upload individual color swatch images for each Belgard product variant. Once a swatch is saved, it replaces the generic product hero on published proposals and in the material picker.

How to source swatches from Belgard.com:
  1. Open any product detail page, e.g. belgard.com/products/pavers/catalina-grana/
  2. Scroll to the Colors section — each color has a small circular swatch
  3. Right-click a swatch → Save Image As… → save with a descriptive name like scandina-gray.jpg
  4. Come back here, find the matching variant, and click Upload swatch
