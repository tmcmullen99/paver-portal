Manage the lifestyle photos for each product. A gallery applies to all color variants of that product — Catalina Grana's photos are shared across all 15 colors. Swatches handle the per-color visual; lifestyle photos handle the product context. Master-only — alters catalog imagery in bulk.

How it works: Click a product card to open the gallery editor. Drag images in to add, drag thumbnails to reorder, click × to remove. Changes propagate to the homeowner catalog and editor materials picker immediately. Existing published proposal pages keep the images they were built with.
