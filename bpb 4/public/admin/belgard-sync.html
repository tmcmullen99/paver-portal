1. Discover products from Belgard

Select which Belgard category pages to scrape. Claude reads each index page and pulls out every product's name, URL, collection, hero image, and description. Each category ≈ 10–20 seconds. Estimated total cost: $0.05–$0.50 depending on coverage.

2. Review discovered products

Each row shows a product Belgard has and whether it matches a row in your local catalog. Status Ready means your catalog has that material but its primary_image_url is currently NULL — approving will write the image. Status Full means the image is already set (won't be overwritten). Status No match means Belgard has this product but it isn't in your local catalog.
