CLIENT ACCOUNT LIFELINE

Resend Paver Portal Portal magic link

For clients who never set up their account and have an unread in-app message — or anyone who needs a fresh sign-in link.

What this does: Looks up the client by email or UUID, generates a one-time sign-in link via Supabase, and emails it to them with a Paver Portal-branded message. If you include a personal note, it appears above the sign-in button. The client lands at /client/dashboard.html with no password needed.

Safe to repeat: If the client already has an active account, no link is sent — you'll see a warning instead.
