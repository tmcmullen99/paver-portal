Do I need a permit for a 4-ft retaining wall in Contra Costa County?
Ask a Pro
Yes — walls over 4 ft measured from the bottom of the footing require a permit and engineered plans there. Under 4 ft with no surcharge is typically exempt. Confirm with the county building department; parcels vary.
